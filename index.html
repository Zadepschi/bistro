About Us

BISTRO BBQ GRILL is a team of professionals who are truly passionate about what they do. Be sure to check out the wine and bar menus created by our head bartender.

Enjoy a modern and inviting interior with large windows, ample natural light, and a minimalist design.

Our open kitchen allows you to watch as your chosen dishes are prepared, making the wait all the more exciting. The open summer terrace could become your favorite spot for lunch, dinner, or a relaxing evening. If you love great food, come visit us – you’re going to love it!
